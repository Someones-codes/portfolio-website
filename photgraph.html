About Me

Hi, I'm Sarah Johnson

With over 10 years of experience in professional photography, I've developed a passion for capturing authentic moments and emotions. My journey began when I received my first camera as a teenager, and since then, I've been dedicated to perfecting my craft.

I specialize in wedding, portrait, and event photography, with a style that blends documentary and fine art approaches. My goal is to create images that tell your unique story in a beautiful, timeless way.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me hiking in nature, experimenting with new recipes, or spending time with my family and two golden retrievers.
