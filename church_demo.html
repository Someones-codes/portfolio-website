Our Mission & Vision

At Grace Community Church, our mission is to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love people, and serve the world. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone can experience the transforming power of God's love.

Founded in 1995, our church has been a cornerstone of the community for over 25 years. We believe in the authority of Scripture, the power of prayer, and the importance of Christian fellowship.

Our vision is to see lives transformed by the gospel, families strengthened, and our community impacted by the love of Christ.
